# Shareable experiments/assays (SEAs) (work in progress)

!!! warning "Work in progress"
    This part of Murmurent is still early and evolving. This page is a
    placeholder; it will be expanded with the SEA catalog format, the
    request/approval flow, and how offerings are declared, once those
    mechanics are settled.

A SEA is a mechanism by which one group offers an experiment, assay,
protocol, or dataset/service to other groups in the centre, and by
which another group requests it. A request crosses group boundaries,
routed by the [Receptionist](agents.md#receptionist) to the right
member on the offering side, but the decision to approve or decline
stays with the offering group's PI.
